Agasteeswarar Temple

First Step

As we were asked to start out journey with blessings of GOD. We choose to visit Agasteeswarar temple in Nungampakkam. We were able to reach there only around noon because of which received blessings without stepping inside the temple premises. We wandered around the place and took a look of the beautiful pond which belongs to that temple. We looked forward for some other temple which is opened at that time but we could not spot one. After a few meters walk we came to Asalathamman Kovil and stood there few mins to have a deep prayer, then we started for the day.
